Area of Specialization

History of Science, especially of Physics.

My main focus is on the history of thermodynamics and statistical mechanics in the late 19th century. I am now studying the works by Ludwig Boltzmann and Josiah Willard Gibbs, in terms of the relation to thermodynamics and physical chemistry.

Position

  • Research Fellow of Japan Society for the Promotion of Science (supervisor: Takehiko Hashimoto at the Department of History and Philosophy of Science, the University of Tokyo)
  • Predoctoral Research Fellow at the Max Planck Institute for the History of Science, Berlin. (funded by the DAAD and JSPS)
  • Ph.D. Candidate at the Department of Philosophy and History of Science, Kyoto University.
