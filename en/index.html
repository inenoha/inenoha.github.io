Hajime Inaba / 稲葉肇

See also Academia.edu and researchmap.

Area of Specialization

History of Science, especially of Physics.

My main focus is on the history of thermodynamics and statistical mechanics in the late 19th century. I am now studying the works by Ludwig Boltzmann and Josiah Willard Gibbs, in terms of the relation to thermodynamics and physical chemistry.
